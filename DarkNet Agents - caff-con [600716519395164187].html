bunni 16-Jul-19 09:00 AM
not at all
coarse-ish ground beans, about what you would do for french press. I start with 1 c of beans, put 6c of cold tap water in to a giant mason jar, dump the grounds in, use a spoon to get the grounds all wet, close jar. After a couple of hours give the jar a gentle shake to get rid of all of the trapped air in the beans, you should see them all float to the bottom. After 24 hours they are "good enough," beyond 72 hours they start to get bitter. Strain through a cheesecloth or nut bag or whatever in to a bottle. Sip on it straight, add ice, or for a more cool summer drink, pour a little in to a cup, add ice and a lot of water.
recommended ratio is between 1:6 and 1:8 from what I've found. 1:6 works well if you can leave it as long as possible, becomes nice and concentrated. We used that ratio because of the volume of our final bottle. that lasts us ~3-5 days, depending.
I'm sure its not as much of a concentrate compared to vac setups, but, its delicious
